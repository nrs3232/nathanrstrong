My Interests

From camping to tennis to board games and more see below for an overview of some of my interests

Going to the beach

   

   

Growing up in Southern California, the beach was always a popular spot for my family and I to visit. I have very fond memories boogieboarding, playing frisbee, looking for sand crabs, bodysurfing and eating packed lunches at the beach.

Click here to learn more about Newport Beach (a favorite beach of ours)

Tennis

   

   

I have played tennis for fun ever since I was a kid. Tennis is a way I have especially connected with my dad since he played tennis in high school. However, I started playing more seriously when I joined my high school's team. Since playing tennis for 3 years in high school, I have taken tennis courses at BYU Provo and have played tennis for fun with friends

Check out this cool tennis video!

Camping

   

   

I love the outdoors and camping! I did boy scouts growing up and went camping almost every month. It is always refreshing to me being in the great outdoors- especially with family and friends. Also, I have made many treasured memories with my dad and younger brother while camping.

Check out this cool video of one of my all time favorite hikes!

Board Games and Card Games

   

   

I love to put my reaction speed, quick thinking, creativity, strategy teamwork and impressions to the test in board games, card games and any group game. I am a bit competitive and love the highs and lows of doing group games. Also, I enjoy a relaxing game here and there as a way to casually conversate in an engaing environment.

Sporting Events

   

   

Sports are a big part of my family and friends. Fall weekends always include at least 1 high school or college football game in person or on TV. We love the high energy environments, in game intensity, pre-game predictions and fun traditions that come with sports.

Travel

   

   

I love to explore the world around me- especially with friends and family. I love to find and see new places and learn about new cultures. Trips are also one of the ways I have bonded with my family.

Swimming

   

   

There's nothing quite like a pool day with family and friends. The pool is a great place to exercise, play games and have a blast. My family would go to the community swimming pool frequently throughout the summer.

Running

   

I always joke that I have a love-hate relationship with running. I got into running in high school when I joined the cross country team my freshman year. Running has connected me with so many friends and has given me so many treasured memories. It has been a great way for me to exercise and thus feel better mentally and physically. However, it can be tiresome and undesirable at times. All in all though, I am grateful for running.

Cooking

   

I love to cook. In fact, in middle school, I took cooking classes with my older cousins for a while. It's always exciting to create something of your own to serve others. I particularly love to prepare omelettes and fajitas.
